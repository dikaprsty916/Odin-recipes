Carrot Cake Cheesecake Bars

With the spiciness of carrot cake and the richness of cheesecake, these carrot cake cheesecake bars are the best of both worlds. These bars do need to chill several hours in the fridge before serving, so make sure to plan ahead.

Ingredients

Brown Butter

  • 11 tablespoons unsalted butter

Cheesecake Filling

  • 8 ounces full fat cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Carrot Cake

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups carrot

Directions

  1. Cook butter until brown and let it cool.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  3. Mix cheesecake ingredients until smooth.
  4. Mix carrot cake ingredients.
  5. Layer batter and swirl.
  6. Bake 40–45 minutes.
  7. Cool and refrigerate before serving.
